    WHAT IS THE DEFINATION OF QURBANI: Qurbani means sacrifice. Every year during the Islamic month of Dhul Hijjah, Muslims around the world slaughter an animal a goat, sheep, cow or camel to reflect the Prophet Ibrahim's willingness to sacrifice his son Ismail, for the sake of God.
